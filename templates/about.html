About NidePizza

Welcome to NidePizza, your number one source for delicious pizzas and fresh sushi. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of food, with an emphasis on quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction.

Our Story

NidePizza was founded in 2024 by a group of food lovers who wanted to bring the authentic taste of Italian pizza and Japanese sushi to the heart of the Netherlands. Since then, our passion for great food and excellent service has helped us grow and delight thousands of customers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer mouth-watering dishes made from the freshest ingredients, prepared by skilled chefs, and delivered to you with a smile. Whether you want a classic Margherita or a bold Dragon Roll, we've got you covered!

Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or want to collaborate with us, feel free to get in touch at contact@nidepizza.com.
